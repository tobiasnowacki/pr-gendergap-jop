*** Replication materials for: 

Fiva, Jon H. and Smith, Daniel M. (2018) "Political Dynasties and the Incumbency Advantage in Party-Centered Environments" in the American Political Science Review.

*** The following four data files are needed to reproduce the results of the paper:

1) FivaSmith_Jan2018.dta 	/* Fiva, J. H. and D. M. Smith (2017): "Norwegian Parliamentary Elections, 1906-2013" Available at: https://search.nsd.no/study/NSD2405 */

2) NorwayMPs.xlsx               /* Archive of Politicians. Available at: http://www.nsd.uib.no/polsys/index.cfm?urlname=storting&lan=&institusjonsnr=1&arkivnr=19&MenuItem=N1_1&ChildItem=&State=collapse  */

3) LastNamesNorway.dta          /* Overview over occurrence of last names in Norway (2013) */

4) USNorway.dta                 /* Comparison of dynasties in US and Norway. Data from D. M. Smith (2018) "Dynasties in Democracies" data set*/

All four data files are included in a zip file which is available through NSD https://search.nsd.no/study/NSD2405
